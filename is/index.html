Free resource

The Icelandic Grammar Reference is completely free to use for students of Icelandic. If you find this to be a helpful resource, why not make a donation?

Donations support the costs of hosting the site and maintaining and expanding the content.

Ever expanding

The Icelandic Grammar Reference is constantly being expanded, updated and refined. The goal is to make it as comprehensive as possible.

Some parts are still a work in progress, so if a section doesn’t seem finished, please check back soon.
